Introduction 

Welcome to the Galactic Conquest game manual!

The GC manual has been designed to give an overview of every aspect of the game, custom controls you need to know, and tips on how to play well.

This manual assumes you have a basic understanding of how to play Battlefield1942, and will not try to teach that. Control descriptions will be releated by what that control does in Battlefield (as each player has custom keys).

The FAQ has answers to common technical questions, as well as common general GC questions.

One important note for new players: Galactic Conquest is a multiplayer game ONLY! We do not directly support bots, or single player mode such as Battlefield 1942 does. This means you will need to play online, or over a LAN network. If you want to try out the vehicles by yourself, simply create your own LAN network game.
