FAQ & Technical Support
  
  
   
 


 
   
Engineers are strongly a supporting class, but very critical to a successful assault or defense. Among other things, the engineer is the only class that can repair vehicles, a very key element to winning (especially on maps that feature large multi-crewed craft). Engineers are also critical on some objective maps, being the only class with Detpacks, which can damage any type of armor.

Key Features:
-----------------
-BUILD SHIELD: Engineers can construct defensive shields once per life. These shield have power to last for several minutes, if the engineer remains close enough to keep an eye on it. Shields completely block all energy based weapons from passing into or out of it's field. Players can run through a shield a minimal damage cost, as can vehicles. Shields do not block grenade type weapons from passing through, but do protect from the splash damage if they are on the other side when the detonate.

-DETPACKS: Engineers carry explosive detonation packs, which can be placed, and then detonated with a remote. These are very effective against all vehicle types, infantry, and required to destroy objectives on GC_Tanaab. Detpacks can be picked up with the Hydrospanner tool.

-Are able to heal vehicles with the Hydrospanner.

-Armed only with a pistol, and vibroblade.

ENGINEERS TIPS:
------------------------
-Don't underestimate the usefulness of a well placed shield. Most classes can't combat the firepower of vehicles at range, but a shield renders a vehicle useless until it approaches, bringing it into range of detpacks, or buying time to capture a base. They also are vital to survive against an air attack on open infantry.

-Shields can be placed to defend turrets, or players, long enough for them to be healed by an Engineer or medic.

-Detpacks are required on Tanaab, to take out the LAB objectives.

-On Taskforce, where the objective is to protect your fleet, and destroy the enemy one, Engineers are VITAL to repair the capital ships. It's recommended almost everyone spawns as one, to repair before going to a station, or launching in a fighter.

-Shields are not available on all maps, nor are detpacks. Specifically, they are disabled for Taskforce and DeathStar, where there is not a use for them, or could hurt the gameplay.
