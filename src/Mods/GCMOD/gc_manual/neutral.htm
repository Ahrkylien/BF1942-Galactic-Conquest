SKIFFS:
Skiffs are found on Tatooine, and server as air transports from base to base. If you are careful not to fall out while moving, you can use your weapons over the rails at other skiffs, or players on the ground. Skiffs come in handy when looking for alternate ways to take Jabba's, or attack the Sarlaac capture point.

-Control with drive controls, climb or decent lower with pitch (or ramp raise/lower keys).

SAIL BARGE:
The Sail barge has a large and powerful deck gun, and two side rail guns for picking off troops.

-Lower position controls side door, that opens to pickup or deploy troops.
-Controls like Skiffs.
-Whichever team controls Jabba's palace, can use the Sail barge as a mobile spawn, so long as it's not crewed by the opposing team at the time.






CLOUD CAR:
The Cloud Car can be stolen for your cause on Bespin, and is used to attack transports or ferry the pilot across platforms. A second crew position is available, that controls the second forward gun.

-Use reverse thrust to hover.

   
   
 


 
   
CLOUD BUS:
A transport vehicle on Bespin, can safely transport up to six players, with room to cram more in if needed. Also is useful as a barricade.

-Fly with normal drive code, raise and lower with pitch (or ramp controls).
